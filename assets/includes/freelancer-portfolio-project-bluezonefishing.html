Bluezone Fishing

A WooCommerce based e-commerce shop for selling of high quality fishing tackle

Bluezone Fishing distribute and supply high quality fishing tackle products personally selected by Neil Bryant, World Champion member of England Boat Fishing Team. We are the sole UK distributors for Artico, Sasame, Shout and Junglegym brands and offer our customers specialist, unique fishing tackle imports. We stock and distribute a vast range of Continental Surf rods, specialist Boat and Match rods as well as the finest Hooks and accessories.
